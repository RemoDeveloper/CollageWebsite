ARTS

The Faculty of Arts houses over 20 departments and interdisciplinary programs and numerous institutes. With approximately 6,000 students, we account for more than one-fifth of the university's total undergraduate program registrations.
Several of our departments are ranked indisputably among the finest in Canada. These include: Drama, the most sought-after theatre school in the country; English, currently ranked 22nd in the world; Philosophy and Sociology, both ranked in the top 100 in the world; as well as Linguistics, History, Psychology, and Modern Languages & Cultural Studies.
